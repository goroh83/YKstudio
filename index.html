About

Violinist Yoon Kim is a graduate of the Cleveland Institute of Music. She has had the privilege to learn from great teachers and performers from major symphony orchestras including Stephen Rose and Elisabeth Adkins.

Through music festivals and professional experience, Ms. Kim has had the opportunity to perform under the baton of renowned conductors in the most prestigious of venues including Carnegie Hall in New York City and Suntory Hall in Tokyo, Japan.

Ms.Kim has eight years of experience performing with the Baltimore Symphony Orchestra as a frequent sub, in addition to nearly two decades as a private instructor.

studio

Ms. Kim maintains a small private studio in Columbia, MD and in Burke, VA. She is an active member of both the American String Teachers Association (ASTA) and the Maryland State Music Teachers Association (MSMTA/ MTNA), and encourages her students to take part in the many enriching programs that they have to offer.

Ms. Kim offers 40 minute and 60 minute lessons, with half hour lessons available for children ages 6 and under, contingent upon a trial lesson to determine compatibility between the teacher, student, and parent.
